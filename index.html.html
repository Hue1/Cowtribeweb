About Us

Our mission is to deliver life-saving animal vaccines and other services where they are needed most and our pioneering innovation makes it possible rain or shine and cheaply! Cowtribe is a technology company based in Ghana. Founded in 2016 the company provide on-demand and subscription based animal vaccine delivery services to last mile farmers in Ghana.
