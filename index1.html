How are we able to deliver the world's best vaccines?

We start with the world's best team

We are a fast-growing, Ghana-based subscription service Cowtribe, which allows farmers to schedule and receive veterinary treatment for livestock and track the health statistics of each animal, was motivated by the founders’ own personal experiences with animals. Both born into a small family of livestock keepers in northern Ghana, they grew up seeing the challenges faced by their parents due to a lack of access to veterinary care!

"This is where we come in: we believe innovation can increase access to better animal health services like vaccines that can dramatically reduce these losses and materially improve the lives of millions (and ultimately billions) of families. So we’re 100% committed to solving this problem, because we live this problem every day,” says co-founder Alima Bawah.
